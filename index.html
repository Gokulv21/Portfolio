About Me

I’m Gokul V, a dedicated technology enthusiast with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems from Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College, Pondicherry. My journey in technology has been marked by hands-on experience and impactful projects. During my time as an intern, I worked on developing the 24/7 Health and Care App, an online health management tool. I also contributed to InfoView, an app designed to enhance communication in schools by sending mandatory notifications to parents and ensuring they are seen and acted upon. This project was particularly notable for its feature that prevented users from bypassing notifications, ensuring timely updates. For my final year project, my team and I developed a Behavioral-Based Biometric Authentication System. This innovative solution enhances security by analyzing users' unique behaviors, such as typing patterns and cursor movements, to verify their identity. If the system detects a discrepancy, it prevents further access and sends an alert. My journey has also been enriched by various programming competitions and hackathons, where I honed my skills in problem-solving and teamwork. In addition to my technical skills, I have a keen interest in content development, software development, and graphic design, including expertise in tools like Photoshop and Illustrator. I’m always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to impactful projects in the tech industry.
